Dr. Stephanie Billingham, Psychotherapist
Welcome to My Practice.

Over the years as a therapist, I’ve helped people create a life that is more content and fulfilling. I work with couples, adults, adolescents, families and children. Whenever I help a child, I involve the parents.

Some of the concerns and struggles that people come to therapy seeking help for include:

  • stress management
  • trauma
  • feelings of depression and anxiety
  • relationship difficulties
  • eating issues
  • low self esteem
  • grief and loss
  • a sense of hopelessness and despair
  • parenting assistance
  • PTSD

Talking together in a non-judgmental way offers an understanding of how these concerns and struggles came about and can lead to better ways of coping with them.

My extensive psychotherapy training has taught me to listen carefully and with compassion, and to be guided by the wishes and needs of each person who comes to see me.

Whatever concerns or issues you may have, my approach is to work with you in a supportive, interactive way to help you change patterns that are not working for you.
